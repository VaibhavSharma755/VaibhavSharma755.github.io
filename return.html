2. TERMS OF CANCELLATION

(a) You can cancel the order prior to the dispatch of Product by Crony or the seller. The order cannot be cancelled once the dispatch has been initiated.

(c) On the receipt of order cancellation request, your order will be cancelled with immediate effect, and the refund process will be initiated within 24 hours of confirmation of cancellation.

(a) You may initiate return of Products within the timelines and as per the terms. We would accept the request for return of such Product subject to the terms of this Policy. Crony will not process return if you have placed the order for a wrong product model, colour or incorrect Product.

(b) You may initiate the return by clicking on the ‘return’ tab in the Order history and selecting the reason for return from the dropdown menu. The ‘return’ tab shall be available only during the duration of the return window. In case of any complain or concern that may arise after the closure of the return window, you may contact the customer care.
